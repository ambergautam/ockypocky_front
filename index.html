Know what’s sparking your child’s interests.

If you ever wonder what’s on your child's mind, just ask me! I’ll help you discover what they love by exploring Antariksh! By tapping on the Ignited, Curious and Star-struck buttons on my galactic map, I will show you what kind of videos are exciting their interest! You can further compare your child’s mind map over time and see how they are evolving. Sounds fun, right?


Set screen time!

I know your kid will love me a little too much! That's why I have a cool timer that lets you fix your child's surfing time. You can set an hourly limit or fix a time of the day - immediately logging out when it's past their viewing time. This way, you can let your kids know when it’s time to stop watching so you don’t have to negotiate!


Let’s play offline!

Save your phone’s data while your kid watches videos on my train! With my Offline feature, download your child’s loved videos so that you don't have to use your phone's data again! That’s right, even without any internet connectivity, my train of videos will keep running forever because there shouldn’t be any pause in your kid’s fun time!
